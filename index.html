4 Learning Engines

One tool for every kind of learning challenge

Switch between engines anytime. Each one is built from the ground up for its specific challenge — not a generic tool with an accessibility patch.

Reading Coach

Built on the Orton-Gillingham method — the gold standard for dyslexia instruction. Every word, every sound, taught the right way.

  • OpenDyslexic & Lexend font — letters that stay put
  • Word-by-word highlighting as text is read aloud
  • Syllable coloring — see how words break apart
  • Adjustable reading speed (50% to 100%)
  • 5 levels of decodable passages or paste your own
  • AI explains any word in simple terms, instantly
  • Phoneme cards for letter-sound practice

Math Coach

Dyscalculia-aware math instruction. Always visual first, always concrete before abstract. Number lines before equations.

  • Interactive number line — see arithmetic as jumps
  • Area model multiplication — no memorizing tables
  • Word problem simplifier — rewritten in plain language
  • Step-by-step breakdown with checkboxes
  • Fraction visualizer with pie charts and bar models
  • AI explains each step in 1–2 sentences max

Writing Coach

Dysgraphia and dyslexia make writing exhausting. SpecialMinds takes the cognitive load off so you can focus on your ideas.

  • Voice-to-text drafting — speak, don't type
  • Sentence frame starters when you're stuck
  • Brain dump → AI organizes your chaos into an outline
  • One paragraph at a time — no overwhelming wall of text
  • Word prediction with phonetic matching
  • Ideas-first feedback — never spelling first

Focus & ADHD Coach

ADHD is a disorder of executive function, not attention. SpecialMinds provides the scaffolding your brain needs — without judgment.

  • Task decomposer — paste any assignment, get micro-steps
  • Pomodoro focus timer with visual progress ring
  • Priority matrix — urgent vs. important, drag and sort
  • Working memory pad — dump thoughts without losing your place
  • Frustration detection — AI offers a break before you burn out
  • Dopamine-friendly completion celebrations

Evidence-Based Design

Every decision backed by decades of research

SpecialMinds isn't built on gut feeling. Every design choice — the font, the color, the sentence length, the feedback tone — comes from peer-reviewed research.

📚

Orton-Gillingham Method

The gold standard for dyslexia instruction, endorsed by the International Dyslexia Association. Structured, sequential, multi-sensory phonics instruction.

🧬

WCAG AAA Accessibility

Every color contrast ratio meets AAA standards. Warm cream backgrounds (#FDF8F0) reduce visual stress for dyslexic readers compared to pure white.

🎯

63% Better Outcomes

NIH-funded research shows 63% better learning outcomes when AI adapts to neurodivergent learning patterns (Zhu et al., 2025).

🧠

Executive Function Science

ADHD task decomposition, Pomodoro timing intervals, and working memory offloading are all validated by CHADD-endorsed research frameworks.
